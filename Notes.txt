What is CDN ? (https://aws.amazon.com/what-is/cdn/)
A content delivery network (CDN) is a network of interconnected 
servers that speeds up webpage loading for data-heavy applications. 
CDN can stand for content delivery network or content distribution network. 
When a user visits a website, data from that website's server has to travel a
cross the internet to reach the user's computer. If the user is located far 
from that server, it will take a long time to load a large file, such as a video 
or website image. Instead, the website content is stored on CDN servers geographically 
closer to the users and reaches their computers much faster.
